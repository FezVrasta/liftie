                                                  A DAY ON THE SKI, IT’S SACRED !

                                                  To maximise the pleasure of skiing, we recommend that you consult the opening condition of the slopes. You can consult this information directly on the website or on the Espace San Bernardo application. Because the state of the slopes is constantly updated, you can know which area to ski in priority. Refer to the piste map to prepare your day’s skiing beforehand to prepare your day’s skiing upstream and find out the state of slopes opening of La Rosière Espace San Bernardo

                                                   

                                                   

                                                  OPENING OF THE SKI LIFTS

                                                  In the mountains, the weather changes quickly, there can also be sun on one side, and fog on the other… The wind is also very variable depending on altitude and orientation. To follow the opening of the lifts and slopes in real time, remember to download the Espace San Bernardo application or use the Interactive Station Planner.
