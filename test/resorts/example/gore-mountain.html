Snow Report

More Terrain Opening!

With trails 3B and Quicksilver coming online, look for over 3.5 miles of skiing and riding today with access from the Adirondack Express II. We are also opening Bear Cub Run today giving beginners a great opportunity to get out on the hill! Please look out for early season conditions and ski and ride with care.

Snowmaking continues on trails Ruby Run, Foxlair, Tahawus and Lower Sleighride as temperatures allow.

Our park crew has an awesome flat rail and a 30′ down rail open on the lower portion of Wild Air. Check out the features and a cool cut from Saturday’s park action here!

Buy tickets online now to lock in your ski dates and enjoy advance purchase savings too!

Podcast

Podcast transcript | December 2, 2021
Hi there skiers and riders, thanks for tuning in. This is Stephanie with your Gore Mountain update for Thursday, December 2 ski day #6. The forecast is looking pretty mixed today with a chance of snow, rain and sleet all before 10am with additional rain and snow predicted for the afternoon. Temps will hover in the upper 30s and lower 40s. Look for a southwest wind at 7 to 10 miles per hour. The forecast isn’t holding us back though as we are looking to open the trails 3B and Quicksilver today! We expect the Bear Cub poma lift as well as Bear Cub Run to also open giving our beginners and never-evers the perfect opportunity to get out on the hill! Freeskiers and snowboarders will find two features open on Wild Air. This will bring our trail count up to 9 with over 3 and a half miles of skiing and riding on a loose granular & machine groomed surface from the top of the Adirondack Express back to the base area. Please keep in mind that early season conditions do exist across the mountain so please ski and ride with care. Snowmaking continues on trails Ruby Run, Foxlair, Tahawus and Lower Sleighride as temperatures allow. If you haven’t already, grab your lift tickets online! The earlier you buy the more you save and quantities are limited so lock in your days ASAP. We have our season passes still available for the best price until December 8th. With unrestricted access any day your pass is valid and loads of additional perks, a season pass offers a great value. Getting ready for the holidays? Give the gift of Gore this year! Check out a fresh new lineup of logo items in our retail shop, we've got awesome souvenirs and gifts ready to go, and passholders save 10% off regular-priced items all season long. Also, we have gift cards available in any denomination that can be spent at Gore, Whiteface and/or Belleayre. Lastly, please familiarize yourself with our latest health and safety protocols in advance of your visit. Masks are required for everyone indoors regardless of vaccination status as well as inside our gondola cabins. They are recommended outdoors for those who are vaccinated – including in lift lines – while unvaccinated individuals are required to mask up at all times except while actively skiing or riding. Head to our Health and Safety page to learn more. Thanks for listening. Have a great day, and we’ll see you on the hill!

What's Happening...

What’s New For 21/22?
Check out snowmaking upgrades and terrain modifications on The Arena and Lower Sunway, new tower guns on Echo, and two additional snowcats. Look for new entrances to Lower Steilhang, Chatterbox Glades, and Cirque Glades (the longest glades in the East!). Buy tickets online now to lock in your dates and save!

Special Notes

Health & Safety Protocols
Masks are required indoors at all times except while you are eating and drinking, and while riding the gondola. Our heated bootup tent is back! While you are outdoors, for vaccinated guests, masks are recommended when social distancing cannot be maintained. For unvaccinated guests, masks are required at all times except while actively skiing or riding. Please refer to our health and safety page on the website to review face covering requirements and additional guidelines in advance of every visit.
