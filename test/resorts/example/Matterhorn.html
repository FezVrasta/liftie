    Matterhorn Ski Paradise

    Open lifts and Pistes

    Are you planning your next skiing or hiking excursion in the Matterhorn Paradise? Good for you! It’s time to find the best lift to take you to the peak of happiness! There’s more than one way to get to the top in Zermatt.
    In Zermatt Bergbahnen’s timetable you’ll find up-to-date information on all the lifts and cable cars operating. No matter whether you want to go to Matterhorn Glacier Paradise or Rothorn, in summer or winter, with skis or hiking boots in your baggage, you’ll find the best way to get to your adventure.

    You'll find today's timetable in the table below and the complete one in the PDF for download.
