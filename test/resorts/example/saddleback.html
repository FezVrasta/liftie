Mountain Update

 

Happy Wednesday! We have a midweek boost on the way with developing snow showers later in the day. This is going to be the nice light and fluffy stuff, leaving up to a 2’’ coating on the snow surface our snowmakers and groomers have been working hard to develop. Temps at the base will top out in the 20s, so bundle up.

 

We officially opened the Rangeley Quad yesterday with access to Upper & Lower Grey Ghost. The Rangeley Quad and The South Branch lift will spin today from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. Stay tuned on Twitter and the mountain report page for further updates on terrain expansion.
 

Stay tuned by following @saddlebackmaine on Twitter and refresh this page for further updates on terrain expansion. Tag us in any of your posts and use the hashtag #lifeisbetterrighthere.

 

Tip of the Day

 

Hey, season pass holders! If you haven’t picked up your season pass, our ticket office is open from 8:00 a.m. to 4 p.m. Midweek is a good time to come grab your pass - the next time you’re at the mountain you can head straight to the lift. 

 

- Molly Shaw
