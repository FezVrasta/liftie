Podcast

Podcast transcript | December 1, 2021
Good Day skiers and riders! This is your Whiteface Insider Snow Cast for Wednesday December 1, 2021. It’s Ski and Ride day 6 of the season! A little snow touched down last night bring our November total to 25”. Snowmaking started is on at our Bear Den Learning Center and our Park crew is setting up for the first full build of the season on Broadway. Today are anticipating 2 lifts and 7 trails open on packed powder & loose granular conditions for intermediate and above skill level skiers and riders. Trail conditions may change throughout the day due to weather and other factors both manmade and natural 2021/2022 Season Passes are on sale now! Lock in the best price by Dec 8th. This year we are in phase II of a three-phase snowmaking improvement project. Phase II brings us new motors and drives in our pump houses and the installation of almost 30,000 feet new snowmaking lines from the 1980 Winter Olympics. The new system allows us higher pressure to move more water to more locations up the mountain. What does that mean? We can make more snow in more places. Whiteface covid policies in place If you are indoors: • Face coverings are required for all guests, regardless of vaccination status. • Face coverings are required in the gondolas at all times. • Face coverings are required in all locker areas. • Face coverings can only be removed indoors when eating or drinking. If you are outdoors: • Vaccinated guests do not need to wear a face covering, but are recommended when near unknown guests, riding lifts with unknown guests, or when unable to socially distance. • Unvaccinated individuals are required to wear face coverings and be socially distanced outside. If you are not feeling well or are experiencing any symptoms of COVID-19, please stay home and plan to visit us another time. Review our health and safety page for more safety information to best prepare for your visit. Thank you for listening, and we’ll see you on the slopes.

Health & Safety

FACE COVERING REQUIREMENTS

If you are indoors:

  • Face coverings are required for all guests, regardless of vaccination status.
  • Face coverings are required in all locker areas.
  • Face coverings can only be removed indoors when eating or drinking.

If you are outdoors:

  • Vaccinated guests do not need to wear a face covering, but are recommended when near unknown guests, riding lifts with unknown guests, or when unable to socially distance.
  • Face coverings are required in the gondolas at all times.
  • Unvaccinated individuals are required to wear face coverings and be socially distanced outside.

If you are not feeling well or are experiencing any symptoms of COVID-19, please stay home and plan to visit us another time.
