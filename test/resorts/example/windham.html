12-4-21

Get ready to kick off the weekend! with cooler temperatures yesterday, our snowmaking team blasted guns all through the night to lay down coverage across the mountain for an epic weekend. Expect 13 open trails today, adding beginner terrain What's Next with the opening of D Lift for the weekend. Check throughout the day and weekend for updates on E carpet and the opening of Wooly Bear.

 

Temperatures are expected to be between 26F - 33F at the base and around 25-27F at the summit. We will have 2 lifts and 1 carpet open today from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

 

Windham Mountain is open seven days a week. Today through Dec 10th are all considered “Green Days” without capacity limitations. Tickets are available at the mountain though we encourage all guests to purchase tickets in advance online. For more information on our Red & Green Day Capacity Calendar, please click here. 

 

Masks are recommended for all guests and staff indoors, regardless of vaccination status, and are required indoors for anyone unvaccinated. Masks are always required, by everyone, in the 3500 Club and Mountain Sports. Masks are not required outdoors. Learn more about our commitment to safety this season and read about our COVID-19 policies here.
